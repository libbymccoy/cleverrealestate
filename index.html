Clever Investor Platform — clickable prototypes

Three views of the platform, mapped to the proposal's build steps: how an agent packages a deal (Step 2), what a matched investor receives (Step 3), and how a seller compares the offers that come back (Step 4). Directional prototypes, not production UI.

Step 2 · Agent view

Deal Package Builder

The agent enters an address (auto-filling specs + valuation), adds condition, photos, and the seller's terms, then matches the home to Clever's vetted investor network.

Open prototype →
Step 3 · Investor view

Investor Deal Blast & Offer

An investor receives the automated deal blast in their inbox, opens the deal on Clever's platform, and submits an offer — or passes with a reason. The platform runs the outreach; the agent never chases buyers.

Open prototype →
Step 4 · Seller view

Compare Your Offers

The seller sees every offer side by side — competing investor offers, proptech cash, and a projected traditional listing — sorts by what matters most, and sends their favorites to their agent. Full transparency, no spam.
